## What Is Linear Regression?

Linear regression is one of the most widely used statistical methods for modeling the relationship between a dependent variable and one or more independent variables. The goal is to find the best-fitting straight line through the data points.

## The Regression Equation

The simple linear regression equation takes the form:

**y = mx + b**

Where:
- **y** is the dependent (response) variable
- **x** is the independent (predictor) variable
- **m** is the slope of the line
- **b** is the y-intercept

## How to Calculate

To find the regression equation, you need to:

1. Calculate the mean of x and y values
2. Compute the deviations from the mean
3. Find the slope (m) using the formula
4. Calculate the intercept (b)
